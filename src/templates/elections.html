About

The 2025 Term 1 By-Election is coming up! This is your chance to join the committee and help run the club!

Becoming a committee member gives you more say in the running of the club, and empowers us to improve the club with your input. Also, it's a great way to develop your leadership and organisation skills, and looks great as a distinguishing feature on your CV! :)

Nominations

Nominations open Wednesday 29th October at 12pm, and close Wednesday 12th November at 12pm.

There are 3 roles open for nominations (see below). You can nominate yourself for as many roles as you like.

You need to have held a paid membership with us for 28 days before you can nominate yourself. If you will not have met this requirement at any point during the nomination window (ie. you bought your membership after October 12th), you can request an exception.

Open positions

The following positions will be open for nominations:

  • Training Officer—responsible for communicating with our coaches and organising sessions!
  • Welfare Officer—main contact for members with welfare concerns. Responsible for our engagement in the TeamUCL Against Hate campaign!
  • Events Officer—responsible for organising trips, socials, and collabs with other groups!

No matter the role, you will be supported by your fellow committee members so that we can all do our very best for the benefit of the club!

Voting

Voting opens Monday 17th November at 12pm, and closes Friday 21st November at 12pm.

You need to have held a paid membership with us for 28 days before you can vote.
