Welcome

Welcome to Parkour Club, the best club in the Union*!

We offer both coached and open sessions every week where we get together to train parkour as a group. See below for our schedule.

After sessions we hold a variety of socials including group dinners, movie and games nights, and themed socials on special occasions. Attending these is a great way form amazing friendships as you connect with fellow members!

All of our sessions are open to everybody, regardless of experience or skill level, so please do come along and give parkour a try! You can get a free Taster membership on our Union page.

*by at least one account :)

Training schedule

Wednesdays 3-5pm: Coached outdoor training in various locations.

Saturdays 3-5pm: Open indoor training at Bloomsbury Fitness.

This schedule applies in Terms 1 and 2 only, excluding Reading weeks. We may also host training and/or social sessions at other times. Please join our WhatsApp community for the most up-to-date information!

Trips

We hold several trips out of London every year. In Terms 1 and 2, we hold trips to other places in the UK to explore their parkour spots. Think Brighton or Sheffield. In the summer we run our annual trip abroad!
